## Description

**Most goal apps give you a to-do list. NorthStar gives you a path.**

NorthStar starts with the bigger question — what does your dream life actually look like? —
then works backward into a roadmap you can walk every single day.

Tell your AI coach where you want to be. It helps you shape that dream into clear goals,
breaks each goal into 3-, 6-, and 12-month milestones, and turns those into the few
non-negotiables that actually move the needle today.

**Why it's different**
• A real roadmap, not a checklist — see the whole journey as a path you're walking down
• Three non-negotiables a day — the opposite of an overwhelming to-do list
• An AI coach in your corner, in the tone you choose: tough-love, balanced, or supportive
• A streak that rewards showing up, not perfection
• An optional vision board to keep the dream vivid
• Your progress syncs securely across devices — pick up anywhere

**How it works**
1. Describe your dream in your own words.
2. Your coach drafts goals, milestones, and stepping stones — edit anything.
3. Each day, knock out three non-negotiables and lock in your streak.
4. Watch the path light up behind you.

No ads. No noise. Just the next right step, every day.

_AI features (coaching suggestions and vision-board images) are optional and use your own
Anthropic / OpenAI API keys._
