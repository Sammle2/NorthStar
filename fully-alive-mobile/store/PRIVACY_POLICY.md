# NorthStar — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 15, 2026_

NorthStar ("we", "us", the "app") helps you turn a long-term dream into daily action.
This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. We've tried to keep
it plain-English.

## What we collect

**Account information.** When you create an account we store your email address and a
display name. Authentication is handled by Supabase (our backend provider).

**Your roadmap data.** The content you create in the app — your dream description, goals,
milestones, stepping stones, daily non-negotiables, streaks, vision-board keywords, and
progress — is stored locally on your device and, once your email is confirmed, backed up
to your private account in our database so you can pick up on another device.

**Optional AI keys.** If you choose to enable AI features, you can paste your own Anthropic
(Claude) and/or OpenAI (DALL·E) API keys. These are stored with your profile and are used
only to make requests to those services on your behalf when you use the related features.

We do **not** collect precise location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers for
advertising. We do **not** show ads. We do **not** sell your data.

## How we use it

- To provide the core app: save and sync your roadmap across your devices.
- To authenticate you and keep your data private to your account.
- To generate AI suggestions and vision-board images **only** when you enable those
  features with your own API keys.

## Who we share it with

We rely on a small number of processors, used only to run the app:

- **Supabase** — database, authentication, and cloud backup of your roadmap.
- **Anthropic** — only if you enable Claude features; your prompts are sent to Anthropic
  using your own key.
- **OpenAI** — only if you enable vision-board image generation; your keywords are sent to
  OpenAI using your own key.

We share data with these providers solely to deliver the features you use, under their
respective terms and privacy policies.

## Data retention and deletion

Your data is retained while your account is active. You can:

- **Reset the app** ("Settings → Reset & start over") to clear local data and sign out.
- **Request account deletion** by emailing us (see below). On request we delete your
  account and the roadmap data associated with it from our database.

## Security

Data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS. Access to your cloud-stored roadmap is restricted
to your authenticated account through database row-level security, so other users cannot
read or modify your data.

## Children

NorthStar is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from
them.

## Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with
a new "last updated" date.
